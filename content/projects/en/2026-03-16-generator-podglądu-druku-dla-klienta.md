problem: Clients often do not understand where the trim size ends, where the
  bleed begins, and which area should remain safe for important content.
solution: The script generates a clear project preview with dimensions, safe
  area/trim/bleed frames, info/warning/danger panels, and an optional right-side
  bleed cut simulation.
impact: " It makes client communication faster, reduces approval
  misunderstandings, and helps explain print production rules in a visual way."
