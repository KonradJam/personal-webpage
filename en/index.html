From perfection in print to automation in IT

Kalisz - Remote / hybrid work

Mistakes cause losses — this is the most valuable lesson I've learned, and one that guides my work over fifteen years in the printing industry. A mistake can cost a lot. Losses are tangible. That's why every file, color, and page must be flawless. Only then can they go to the printing press. Working in a printing house, I reached perhaps the highest level of my perceptiveness, accuracy, and composure. And it is precision — not tools or technology — that proved to be my greatest asset when I started programming and automating processes.

My fascination with automation came naturally. Over time, I became convinced that repetitive tasks should be performed by machines — if only so that people have more time for creative work, or simply for living. So I started building tools that combine programming logic with DTP precision, which speed up production and eliminate human error. Outside of automation, I experiment with electronics and microcontrollers — it's my private laboratory where code meets the physical world.

If I can help you with anything, I'd be happy to collaborate in Kalisz or remotely.

Give routine to algorithms. Keep what matters to yourself.
